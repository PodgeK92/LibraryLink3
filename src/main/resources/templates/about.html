About LibraryLink

Welcome to LibraryLink, your go-to platform for managing your library's resources efficiently. Whether you're a librarian, member, or administrator, LibraryLink provides the tools you need to streamline your library operations.

Our Mission

At LibraryLink, our mission is to empower libraries to serve their communities by providing innovative and user-friendly solutions for managing library resources.

Our Team

LibraryLink is developed and maintained by a dedicated team of student software engineers who are passionate about improving library services.
